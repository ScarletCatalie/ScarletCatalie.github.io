hey. i'm natalie clayton.

I writes words about video games online, for money. Fancy that.

You can find most of my writing in Rock Paper Shotgun and VG247, but I've also graced Kotaku, PC Gamer, PCGamesN, Eurogamer and Polygon with well-good words. There's a lot of it, so I've gone and highlighted the best bits over here

GamesIndustry.Biz once called me a Rising Star in 2018. A MAZE Berlin / Humble shortlisted me for New Talent the same year. I thinks that's pretty cool.
