hey. i'm nat. from online.

This is a collection of words, games and "stuff" created by Edinburgh-based games person Natalie Clayton.

My writing has popped up in over a dozen sites, including Rock Paper Shotgun, VG247, Kotaku, PC Gamer, PCGamesN, Eurogamer and Polygon. You might have also caught my public speaking at A MAZE Berlin, Ludicious, Videobrains or elsewhere.

I develop tiny games about queers and robots and frogs. The best of these is existentially-horrifying mech drama Can Androids Pray. My grubby fingerprints have also touched bigger stuff, like Alien: Isolation and Minecraft: Console Editions.

GamesIndustry.Biz once called me a Rising Star in 2018. A MAZE Berlin / Humble shortlisted me for New Talent the same year. I think that's pretty cool.

Here's my face.
