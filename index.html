hey. i'm natalie clayton.

Nat writes words about video games online, for money. Fancy that.

You can find her writing in Rock Paper Shotgun, VG247, Kotaku, PC Gamer, PCGamesN, Eurogamer and Polygon. There a lot of it, so she's only gone and highlighted the best bits over here

Nat also made a few tiny games, including existentially-horrifying mech drama Can Androids Pray. Her grubby mitts have also touched bigger titles like Alien: Isolation and Minecraft: Console Editions.

GamesIndustry.Biz once called her a Rising Star in 2018. A MAZE Berlin / Humble shortlisted her for New Talent the same year. She thinks that's pretty cool.
